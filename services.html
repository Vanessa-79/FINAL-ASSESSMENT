Services Offered

Vanessa cake bakery is an establishment that produces and sells cakes. We provide services for special occasions such as weddings,anniversaries,birthday parties and baby shower cakes. We offer a rotating selection of popular desserts and flavors during retail hours. Special flavors offered are vanilla,strawberry,pineapple,chocolate.The products being sold in the bakery are various desserts and cakes. Catering and deliveries is also available under some circumstances.

We measure and combine ingredients using mixers,ovens, and other eqipment to make cakes. We also greet customers,answer questions,accepting orders and payments and providing exceptional customer service.
